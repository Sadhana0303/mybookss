# C in Depth


          Writer:S.K. Srivastava and Deepali Srivastava

        Summary of Content:The book can be utilized by both beginners and advanced level programmers as a self-evaluation and learning source.
         The book begins with an introduction to the languages and explains the elements, inputs and output, and operator and expressions used in 
         programming. Control statements, functions, arrays, pointers, strings and files are covered next in successive chapters. Every chapter has examples

# Data Structure through C in Depth 

       Writer:S.K. Srivastava and Deepali Srivastava

        Summary of Content:The book Data Structures Through C in Depth is primarily meant for computer science students who are learning C language. 
        The book explains the essential components of C language through the simplest ways, with illustrative examples. Ten chapters along with important questions
        and their brief answers are provided in the book.Gradually, readers are approached with different topics like stack, queue, linked lists, recursion,
        sorting, tress, searching, hashing and graphs. Students are greatly benefited as they gain a better understanding of crucial concepts.
